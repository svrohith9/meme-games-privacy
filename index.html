Privacy Policy

Applies to: Brainrot Beatbox, Soundboard Royale, Walk It Off, Call & Response, and Era Wars (the "Games"). Last updated: April 18, 2026.

p>

Short version

These Games do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. They run entirely on your device. No account, no sign-in, no analytics, no ads, no trackers, no network calls for gameplay.

What data is collected

None. The Games do not request or transmit any personal information.

What is stored on your device

Gameplay progress, settings, high scores, unlocked cosmetics, and user-typed content (such as the Problem Locker in Walk It Off) are stored locally on your device using Android's standard local storage (SharedPreferences / local files). This data never leaves your device. Uninstalling the app removes it.

Permissions

The Games use only permissions necessary for local functionality (for example, vibration for haptic feedback). The Games do not request Internet, Location, Camera, Microphone, Contacts, or Storage-access permissions for anything beyond their own private app storage.

Children

The Games are intended for a general audience aged 13 and older. No personal information is collected from any user, including children.

Third-party services

None. The Games include no advertising SDKs, no analytics SDKs, no crash-reporting SDKs, and no third-party trackers.

Sharing content

When you use a "share" feature in a Game, an image is generated locally on your device and passed to Android's system share sheet. You choose the destination. The Game itself does not upload anything to any server.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.
